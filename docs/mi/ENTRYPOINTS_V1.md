\# MI EntryPoints v1



These are the ONLY allowed business actions in Phase 1.



---



\## build\_store

Purpose:

Create a complete first draft of a business.



MI Responsibilities:

\- Understand business intent

\- Generate products/services

\- Assign categories \& tags

\- Generate images

\- Prepare screen-ready content



UI Role:

\- Collect intent

\- Show progress

\- Review output



---



\## fix\_catalog

Purpose:

Resolve gaps in products (tags, categories, images).



MI Responsibilities:

\- Detect gaps

\- Apply fixes in bulk

\- Track confidence



UI Role:

\- Trigger fix

\- Review changes

\- Override selectively



---



\## prepare\_publish

Purpose:

Determine if a store is ready to publish.



MI Responsibilities:

\- Evaluate completeness

\- Evaluate quality

\- Set publishReady belief



UI Role:

\- Display readiness

\- Explain reasons



---



\## generate\_promo

Purpose:

Create promotional content.



MI Responsibilities:

\- Choose targets

\- Generate creatives

\- Prepare deployment



UI Role:

\- Approve or adjust



---



\## refresh\_understanding

Purpose:

Re-evaluate the business when inputs change.



MI Responsibilities:

\- Update beliefs

\- Recalculate gaps

\- Suggest next actions
